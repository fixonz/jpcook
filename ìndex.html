Meet Our Executive Chef

Elliot Johnson-Paul, Executive Chef and owner of JP Dining, has a distinguished culinary background. Starting at 15 years old, he trained at the renowned Petersham Hotel, later earning prestigious scholarships and training at the world-famous Intercontinental Park Lane. His culinary expertise spans across modern British cuisine, with a strong influence from his Anglo-Indian heritage. Elliot has worked at Claridge’s Hotel and established his own company, JP Dining, which now serves high-profile clients worldwide. His work has been featured for royalty and UHNWI, making every dish an extraordinary experience.
